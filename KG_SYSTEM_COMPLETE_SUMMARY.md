2. **医生问诊** (inquiry)
3. **患者描述** (description)

5. **患者确认** (confirmation)
6. **检查单** (test_order)
7. **患者同意** (test_agreement)
8. **治疗建议** (treatment)
9. **患者询问** (treatment_inquiry)
